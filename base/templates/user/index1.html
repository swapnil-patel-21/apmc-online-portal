APMC Online Portal

APMC Online Portal is a portal which takes information of different types of commodities details from users and give an option to booking a slot as per their convenience. This process reduces the time. It also reduces the man power requirement. A portal is used to analyze price trends, arrival and trading activities. Users can also check their past history. It helps the farmer to sell their product at high price. A portal will provide transparent and secure platform for farmers.
